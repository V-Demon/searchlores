---

# RFC-0011

# LoreForge Specification

Version: Draft 0.1

Status: Experimental

Author: Searchlores Academy

---

# Abstract

LoreForge defines the creation, evolution, validation, publication and governance of Lores.

A Lore is not merely configuration.

A Lore is an investigative methodology encoded as an artifact.

LoreForge provides the tools required to:

* create Lores
* improve Lores
* compare Lores
* audit Lores
* publish Lores
* inherit from Lores

The objective is to transform investigative knowledge into reusable structures.

---

# 1. Core Principle

Searchlores executes investigations.

LoreForge creates investigators.

---

A prompt defines:

```text
What to ask.
```

A Lore defines:

```text
How to search.
```

---

# 2. Lore Definition

A Lore represents:

```text
Investigation Knowledge
```

encoded in machine-readable form.

Examples:

```text
llm.lore

malware.lore

cryptography.lore

economics.lore

history.lore

worldbuilding.lore
```

---

# 3. Lore Lifecycle

Every Lore follows:

```text
DRAFT
   ↓

EXPERIMENTAL
   ↓

VALIDATED
   ↓

PUBLISHED
   ↓

MAINTAINED
   ↓

ARCHIVED
```

---

# 4. Lore Identity

Every Lore SHALL possess:

```yaml
lore:

  id:

  name:

  version:

  author:

  created:

  updated:
```

---

# 5. Lore Metadata

Example:

```yaml
metadata:

  category:

  tags:

  domains:

  maturity:

  license:
```

---

# 6. Lore Design Goals

A good Lore SHOULD:

* reveal assumptions
* reveal contradictions
* expand inquiry
* resist dogma
* remain domain aware

A Lore SHOULD NOT:

* impose conclusions
* optimize persuasion
* suppress alternatives

---

# 7. Lore Composition

A Lore is assembled from modules.

```yaml
modules:

  assumptions

  narratives

  contradictions

  vectors

  sources

  questions
```

---

# 8. Lore Templates

LoreForge SHALL support templates.

Examples:

```text

Historical Research

Philosophical Inquiry

Narrative Analysis
```

---

# 9. Lore Inheritance

Lores MAY inherit.



# 29. The LoreForge Principle

A Lore is not software.

A Lore is encoded experience.

A Lore is a reusable investigation tradition.

# 30. Philosophy

Knowledge is difficult to transfer.

Methods are easier.

Searchlores does not merely preserve discoveries.

Searchlores preserves ways of discovering.

LoreForge is the mechanism through which investigative culture becomes reusable.
