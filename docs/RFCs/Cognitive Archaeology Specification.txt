# RFC-0007

# Cognitive Archaeology Specification

### Version: Draft 0.1

---

# Abstract

Cognitive Archaeology is the systematic reconstruction of hidden conceptual structures.

Its purpose is not to analyze statements.

Its purpose is to analyze the concepts that make statements possible.

Cognitive Archaeology treats concepts as archaeological sites.

---

# 1. Core Principle

Most investigation systems analyze:

```text id="1x3b2t"
Facts
Claims
Sources
Arguments
```

Searchlores additionally analyzes:

```text id="90zvmy"
Concepts
```

Because concepts determine what can be thought.

---

# 2. Fundamental Assumption

Every concept contains:

```text id="sxebjs"
Definitions

Assumptions

Boundaries

Contradictions

Genealogies

Alternatives
```

The concept itself becomes the investigation target.

---

# 3. Concept Artifact

Definition:

A concept selected for investigation.

Examples:

```text id="lfbspw"
Intelligence

Understanding

Expertise

Progress

Security

Freedom

Innovation

Objectivity
```

---

# 4. Cognitive Excavation

The engine SHALL perform:

```text id="j32xol"
Identification

Decomposition

Genealogy

Contradiction Analysis

Boundary Mapping

Alternative Reconstruction
```

---

# 5. Concept Decomposition

Input:


# 9. Boundary Analysis

À partir de maintenant, Searchlores n'est plus simplement un système d'investigation.

Il devient une sorte de :

```text id="j6y06u"

+
OSINT conceptuel

+
Reverse Engineering cognitif
```

```text id="1rjlwm"

        ▼

Narrative Archaeology

        ▼

Cognitive Archaeology

        ▼

Ontology Builder
```

## Ce que je proposerais ensuite

Avant d'écrire une seule ligne de Python, il manque encore un document fondamental :

```text id="4dzk4r"
RFC-0008
Ontology Builder
```

Parce que les RFC-0005 à RFC-0007 produisent des fragments :

* concepts
* récits
* hypothèses
* contradictions

L'Ontology Builder serait le premier composant capable de les fusionner en une **carte cohérente du domaine étudié**.

Autrement dit :

> Les RFC précédents apprennent à fouiller.
>
> RFC-0008 apprendra à construire la cité à partir des ruines.
